About the Green Power Ranger

The Green Power Ranger, also known as Tommy Oliver, is one of the most iconic characters in the Power Rangers franchise. His journey from villain to hero is a story of redemption and strength.

Originally introduced in Mighty Morphin Power Rangers, Tommy has become a fan favorite, showcasing exceptional martial arts skills and leadership qualities.

Join us as we explore the origins and adventures of this beloved character!

Facts About the Show

The Power Rangers franchise began in 1993 and has spawned numerous series and films. The original team featured five teenagers who were given powers to protect the world from evil.

Tommy first appeared as the Green Ranger in Season 1 and quickly became a vital member of the team.

The character has had multiple transformations, including the White Ranger and the Red Zeo Ranger.
